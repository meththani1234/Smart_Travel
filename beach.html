Beach Activities - Sri Lanka

Popular Beach Destinations

  • Mirissa: Famous for whale watching and golden sands.
  • Unawatuna: Great for swimming, snorkeling, and beach bars.
  • Arugam Bay: World-class surfing destination.
  • Bentota: Perfect for water sports like jet skiing and banana boating.
