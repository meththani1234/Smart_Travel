Hiking & Nature - Sri Lanka

Top Hiking & Nature Destinations

  • Adam's Peak (Sri Pada): Famous pilgrimage site with stunning sunrise views.
  • Ella Rock: Scenic hike with panoramic views of the hill country.
  • Horton Plains & World's End: Misty plateau with dramatic cliff drop-offs.
  • Knuckles Mountain Range: UNESCO site with diverse wildlife and lush forests.
