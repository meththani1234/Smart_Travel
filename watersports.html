Water Sports & Diving - Sri Lanka

Top Water Sports & Diving Destinations

  • Hikkaduwa: Snorkeling and diving with vibrant coral reefs and marine life.
  • Unawatuna: Calm beaches great for kayaking and paddleboarding.
  • Trincomalee: Famous for whale watching, scuba diving, and snorkeling.
  • Kalpitiya: Known for kite surfing and dolphin watching.
